About Us

What we do

You can access your seating information by logging into your account on our platform. Once logged in, you'll find details about your upcoming exams, including your assigned seat and exam hall.

In most cases, seat assignments are final. However, if you have a valid reason for requesting a change, please contact our support team. We will consider such requests on a case-by-case basis.

Teachers can access a dedicated portal on our platform to efficiently manage exams. They can allocate seating, monitor students during exams, and receive real-time updates. If you are a teacher, you can contact our support team for more information on using this feature.
